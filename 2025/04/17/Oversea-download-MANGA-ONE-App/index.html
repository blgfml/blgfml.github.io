マンガワン(MANGA ONE)

Benefits:



  • Red heart (free life): unlock chapters (except the latest chapter.).

  • Green heart (sp life): unlock latest chapters or extra chapters.

    - Green heart (sp life): Can be refilled by logging in daily or watching an ad video(2 times/day).

  • Yellow coin (purchased coins): Used to buy the manga VOL.


Caution!

  1. Age above 19.
  2. Not risk-free.
  3. Google only allows users to change their country setting in the Google Play Store once every 12 months.

Preparation

  1. Own bank card. (To set up a new country or region, you must have a payment method.)
  2. Create a new google account with only basic info. (You can’t use an existing account as it contains your actual location information)

Download VPN

  1. Free 1hr Japan VPN: VPN Japan - get Japanese IP. ()
  2. Open and run the Japan VPN, connect to a Japan server.

Create a Japan Google Play Account (DETAILS)

  1. Clear data and cache of Google Play Store

    Settings > Apps > Google Play Store > Storage > Clear data > clear cache
  2. Log in new google account into your device.

    Settings > Google > Add another account > Follow the instruction
    • You can’t use an existing account as it contains your actual location information
  3. REQUIRE a payment method from the new country.
    • To set up a new country or region, you must have a payment method.
    • Add credit or debit card. (Not risk-free)
  4. Once the page turn Japanese, it’s successful!

Download Manga One

  1. Search “Manga One”
  1. Download
  2. Log In/Sign Up

Top Up

Delete payment method.

  1. Google Play Store
    • Profile (Top right) > Payments and subscriptions > Payment methods > More payment settiings
  1. Google account
    • REMOVE the cards.

Enjoy the manga! :)
